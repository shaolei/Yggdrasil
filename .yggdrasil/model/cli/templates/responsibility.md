# Templates Responsibility

Default templates and platform rules — used by `yg init`.

**In scope:**

- default-config.ts: default config.yaml content
- platform.ts: mapping platform (cursor, claude-code, copilot, cline, roocode, codex, windsurf, aider, gemini, amp, generic) to rules paths
- rules.ts: Yggdrasil rules content for agents
- graph-templates/: node, module, service, library templates (copied to .yggdrasil/templates/)

**Out of scope:**

- Init logic (cli/commands)
- Templates in .yggdrasil/templates/ (generated by init, not part of source)
